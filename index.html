Theater in Nature is a cultural multi comprehensive activity project on a 6000 square feet land among olive trees in Italy

Open and green

Teatriamo

By the name of 'Teatriamo' is intended a permanent theater workshop of all aspects of staging a show: artistic, technical, video, administrative, marketing. Objective of Teatriamo is not only to train physically and intellectually for the Theatre, but to promote the art of communicating with respect and style, to relaunch good speech, group work, history and memory, group creativity, relaxation and, through the role study: self-analysis.

Open to host guests

Located 30 km from Rome and 5 km from the small town of Palombara Sabina, Theatre in Nature includes a 100mq Theater tent (Pagoda) with a low stage for indoor activities, wooden guest houses, bathrooms, common kitchen and living room with WiFi, and a wide clear space for outdoor activities with 60 olive trees, 6 fruit trees, a swimming pool and a small wood very inspiring for performances.
There are no buildings around: the whole area is a plunge in the greenest nature.
